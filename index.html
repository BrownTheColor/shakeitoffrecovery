Supportive and Safe Enviorment

A safe and supportive environment with programs dedicated to serve the adult male and female population in Arizona with behavioral health and substance abuse need. Residents are able to build self-care skills through participation in onsite and community services. Some residents may be mandated by “certain” courts.

Contact us

24/7 Nursing and Care

A 24/7 nurse on call. Licensed RN provides wellness check 1 to 2 times a week or as needed. Rn provides regular medication check and education to patients and staff. Rn provides an instructed exercise regime for clients struggling with physical and mental needs. A
nutritionist will provide weekly/monthly meal plans. Licensed nutritionist provides individualized meal plans for those in need of this serve due to health concerns.

Contact us

Homeless services

One of our main goals at Shake It off Recovery is to get the vulnerable rehabilitated and off the street and away from unhealthy lifestyle. We provide housing and supportive services to assist in the return of independent living as soon as treatment completed and/or resident feels ready.

About Us!

Shake IT Off recovery LLC is a behavioral health residential facility serving all people most venerable in the Arizona community. Our goal is to help meet the mental, physical needs and teach how to achieve theses goals oneself. A licensed residential facility, that feels like home. A comfortable and safe environment created to build self-confidence, self-discipline and self-reliance. A group home with a caring atmosphere and staff to help facilitate resident build skills, develop healthy relationships and learn a new way to cope, as well as, manage symptoms. The resident has the opportunity to strengthen these skills in a supportive environment before transitioning into an independent lifestyle. This behavioral health treatment facility provides structures and supervision. With a licensed registered nurse offering her services ones a week on call 24/7.
